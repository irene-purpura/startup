Smart Guide for Businesses

Join the future of in-store navigation and offer your customers a faster, smoother, and smarter shopping experience.

Why Join ShelfGuide?

ShelfGuide is building the next generation of in-store navigation. Businesses that join our network gain early access to innovative tools that improve customer flow and enhance the shopping experience.

By consenting to map and track your store layout, you allow our app to guide customers efficiently through your aisles - making your store more appealing and easier to navigate.

Benefits for Retailers

Attract More Shoppers

When your store is available on the ShelfGuide app, customers can find what they need faster - increasing satisfaction and encouraging repeat visits.

Improve Store Flow

Smart navigation reduces congestion and helps distribute foot traffic evenly across the store.

Gain Data Insights

Access optional, privacy-friendly insights into store movement patterns and product-finding behavior.
