The problem

Every day, millions of people waste time wandering through supermarkets trying to find products. Aisles change, products get moved, and even the simplest shopping trip becomes slow and stressful. What should take 10 minutes often becomes a 45-minute search.

Our solution

Shelf Guide turns grocery shopping into a simple, fast, and guided experience. Users upload their shopping list—typed, spoken, or photographed—and if the store is registered in our system, our AI instantly reorganizes the list based on the real layout of that supermarket. The result? A fully optimized route that helps you find everything in minutes.

How it works

1. Create your list

Upload your list in text, voice, or photo format.

2. Store data integrated

The store provides its layout and shelf structure when joining the platform.

3. Get your optimized route

The AI arranges your list in the perfect walking order and guides you step-by-step.

For Businesses

Stores that join Shelf Guide become visible to thousands of shoppers looking for fast, optimized routes. By sharing their store layout, they enable a smoother shopping experience, attract more customers, reduce in-store search time, and bring innovation to their supermarket.
